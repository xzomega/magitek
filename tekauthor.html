About the Author

xzomega, known as Timothy outside of the digital realm, is an aspiring coder in his mid-thirties. He has opted to pursue this path after spending his working career in the customer service/hospitality industry and welcomes the challenge and change of pace that coding brings to his life. The author originates from the Midwestern part of the United States but currently resides in the Southeastern region where beaches and Palmetto Trees are abundant.
